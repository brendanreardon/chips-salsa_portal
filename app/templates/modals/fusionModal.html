Optional Field: Fusions from RNA

We recommend STAR Fusion to call somatic fusions from RNA. The Molecular Oncology Almanac specifically requires the following columns:

#fusion_name, SpanningFrags, LeftBreakpoint, RightBreakpoint

Example upload file for fusions using required columns:
#fusion_name SpanningFrags LeftBreakpoint RightBreakpoint
EML4--ALK 0 6:47471176 11:66563752
COL1A2--APBA3 6 9:35657873 21:46320255
POLR2A--AP2M1 12 17:7406801 3:183898675
