Optional Field: Insertions & Deletions

If providing insertions and deletions (InDels) for upload, they should follow the guidelines set by the NCI's Mutation Annotation Format (MAF). We recommend Strelka and Oncotator to generate this file. The Molecular Oncology Almanac specifically requires the following columns:

Hugo_Symbol, NCBI_Build, Chromosome, Start_position, End_position, Reference_Allele, Tumor_Seq_Allele1, Tumor_Seq_Allele2, Variant_Classification, Protein_Change, Tumor_Sample_Barcode, Matched_Norm_Sample_Barcode, Annotation_Transcript,
t_ref_count, t_alt_count
